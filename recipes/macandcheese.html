Mac and Cheese

Description

A dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar. It can also incorporate other ingredients, such as breadcrumbs or meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dried macaroni
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 4 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Add the macaroni, water, and salt to the Instant Pot and stir to combine. Close the lid and set to pressure cook on high for 4 minutes. Once the timer goes off, set the Instant Pot to quick release.
  2. Remove the lid, add the butter, and stir until melted. Add the cheddar cheese, then the milk, 1 tablespoon at a time, and stir until melted and creamy.
  3. Garnish with parsley
  4. Enjoy!
